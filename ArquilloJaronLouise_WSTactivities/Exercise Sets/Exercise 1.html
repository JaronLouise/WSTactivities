Bulking Phase Meal Plan

Here is a list of healthy and delicious meals for gaining muscle mass

Weekly Meal Plan:

  1. Monday
    1. Breakfast
      • 6 egg omelette with spinach (approx. 564 kcal)
      • Large portion of granola, whole milk, and sliced banana (approx. 750 kcal)
    2. Lunch
      • Double chicken breast, broccoli, and rice (approx. 700 kcal)
      • Tinned tuna, quinoa, avocado, and broccoli (approx. 500 kcal)
    3. Dinner
      • Tuna steak with olive oil, two sweet potatoes, and quinoa (approx. 800 kcal)
      • Beef Jerky (approx. 410 kcal)
  2. Tuesday
    1. Breakfast
      • 1 or 2 bagels with peanut butter (approx. 380/760 kcal)
      • Scrambled eggs with cheese and spinach
    2. Lunch
      • Grilled salmon, brown rice, and mixed veggies (approx. 650 kcal)
      • Chicken and avocado wrap (approx. 600 kcal)
    3. Dinner
      • Stir-fried beef with vegetables and rice (approx. 800 kcal)
      • Greek yogurt with mixed berries and nuts (approx. 400 kcal)
  3. Wednesday
    1. Breakfast
      • Overnight oats with chia seeds, nuts, and honey (approx. 500 kcal)
      • Protein smoothie with banana, spinach, and almond butter (approx. 600 kcal)
    2. Lunch
      • Grilled chicken sandwich on whole grain bread (approx. 550 kcal)
      • Quinoa salad with chickpeas, veggies, and feta cheese (approx. 600 kcal)
    3. Dinner
      • Turkey meatballs with whole wheat pasta and marinara sauce (approx. 750 kcal)
      • Mixed greens with olive oil and grilled tofu (approx. 400 kcal)
  4. Thursday
    1. Breakfast
      • Oatmeal with nuts, seeds, and a drizzle of maple syrup (approx. 500 kcal)
      • Whole grain toast with avocado and poached eggs (approx. 400 kcal)
    2. Lunch
      • Chicken stir-fry with brown rice and veggies (approx. 650 kcal)
      • Quinoa bowl with black beans, corn, and salsa (approx. 600 kcal)
    3. Dinner
      • Grilled shrimp with sweet potato and broccoli (approx. 700 kcal)
      • Cottage cheese with sliced fruit and honey (approx. 350 kcal)
  5. Friday
    1. Breakfast
      • Pancakes made with protein powder and blueberries (approx. 600 kcal)
      • Fruit smoothie with spinach, protein powder, and almond milk (approx. 500 kcal)
    2. Lunch
      • Steak sandwich with roasted veggies (approx. 700 kcal)
      • Chicken Caesar salad with whole grain croutons (approx. 600 kcal)
    3. Dinner
      • Baked salmon with quinoa and green beans (approx. 800 kcal)
      • Mixed nuts and dried fruit (approx. 300 kcal)
  6. Saturday
    1. Breakfast
      • French toast with almond butter and berries (approx. 600 kcal)
      • Yogurt parfait with granola and mixed fruits (approx. 450 kcal)
    2. Lunch
      • Beef burrito with brown rice, beans, and salsa (approx. 700 kcal)
      • Spinach and feta cheese stuffed chicken breast (approx. 600 kcal)
    3. Dinner
      • Lamb chops with mashed potatoes and asparagus (approx. 800 kcal)
      • Roasted chickpeas and hummus (approx. 350 kcal)
  7. Sunday
    1. Breakfast
      • Smoothie bowl with acai, nuts, and seeds (approx. 500 kcal)
      • Egg and sausage muffin (approx. 400 kcal)
    2. Lunch
      • Chicken fajitas with bell peppers and tortillas (approx. 700 kcal)
      • Vegetarian pizza on whole grain crust (approx. 650 kcal)
    3. Dinner
      • BBQ ribs with roasted potatoes and coleslaw (approx. 900 kcal)
      • Grilled vegetable skewers with couscous (approx. 450 kcal)
